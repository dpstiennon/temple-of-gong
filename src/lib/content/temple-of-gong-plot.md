# Temple of Gong - Plot Outline

## Plot Beats

### The Temple Setting
- The temple has gongs everywhere. Brass and bronze, large and small. Each monk has a gong hanging from his neck.
- The temple is silent until prayer time begins, in which case the noise of monks hammering on gongs is deafening.

### The Monks
- All the monks of gong are mostly deaf.
- A few can write English, and communicate via chalk on a blackboard.
- The monks are friendly at first. They offer hospitality of the temple.

### The Tour
- There should be a tour available, where they show you the inner building.
- Most prominently, the central temple contains an enormous idol of Gong, their monkey God.
- The idol is a huge brass Monkey with 50 foot cymbals held apart in its hands.

### The Bell Trap
- If at any page with monks present you use the bell item, the monks immediately fling you into the prison cells beneath the temple.

### Finding Jonah
- By sneaking around the monastery at night, you can discover Jonah imprisoned.
- He made the same mistake above - the monks imprisoned him for bringing a bell, which they consider blasphemous.

### Prison Escape Options
- If you find yourself in prison, you can dig out using the Titanium Spork.
- However, if you choose the default of "wait", the monks fling you and Jonah both off the cliff the next day.
- You can rescue Jonah from prison either by:
  1. Ending up in prison yourself and digging both of you out with the titanium spork
  2. Distracting the monks with a comic book and stealing the key to Jonah's cell

### The Escape
- When you escape, the alarm sounds.
- Furious monks begin winding the idol of Gong.
- When its cymbals clash, it causes avalanches in the surrounding mountains.
- If you try to run down the path by which you came, you will be crushed by an avalanche.
- Jonah will offer an alternative route.

### The Zeppelin
- Jonah has in his possession a pocket Zeppelin (it's meant to be silly).
- If he's able to obtain it and inflate it, he will be able to fly both of you out.

### The Fall to Shangri-La
- If you take this route, it should almost go right... but you become separated from Jonah and fall to the valley below.
- You survive, and find yourself in the mysterious city of Shangri-La.
- The monks of gong have sealed off Shangri-La from the rest of the world with their avalanches.

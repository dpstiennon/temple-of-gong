The titanium edge slides into the gap between the bolt and the frame. You lean on it. Something groans. You lean harder, and the latch pops open with a clang.

Jonah watches with wide eyes as you kneel beside him and work the spork's tine into the corroded chain pin. 

"Is that a titanium spork?"

The pin resists, then doesn't. One wrist free. Then the other.

"Yes!" he laughs. "You remembered!  I taught you well.  On any adventure, always bring a box of matches, a comic book, and a titanium spork."
